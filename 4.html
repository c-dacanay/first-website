Gaia Online
2003 -

Gaia Online is an anime linklist turned community forum turned MMORPG.
"Gaians" could use Gaia Gold to buy early 2000s-flavored clothes for their avatars.
It had a robust economy that crashed spectacularly in 2013.

I had a lot of firsts on Gaia.
I played my first MUD, had my first "job" (colorist), and launched my first "company".
