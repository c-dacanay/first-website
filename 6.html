Surreal*Twilight
2001 - 2013

A roleplaying forum focused on literacy and open storytelling.
S*T was run and organized by a group of 14-20 year olds.
In 2003 it was the #1 roleplaying forum according to Neopet's Anime Guild and had around 11k active members.

I was an active member, moderator, and eventually admin at 13.
It was a corner of the internet that really belonged to us.
(don't give a 13 year old admin privileges to anything though)
